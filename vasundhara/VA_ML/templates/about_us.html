ABOUT US

 

In this website we provide a profound way of growing the crops in more efficient manner and in a specific time. We help farmers to grow their crops in more optimal way maximize the capacity of their soil. This website will create a report based on the given information by the farmer/user in the data filling form contains following columns such as: Soil type, Soil nutrients like: Magnesium, Potassium, Calcium carbonate, Ph level.

The user simply have to fill up a form that contains the details about the soil's nutrition. This provided information will create a report about the quality of the soil and what kind of crops can be grown in that soil. The website will have backend machine learning approach to generate the artificial intelligence for deriving the best optimal solution for the farmer. The user can also enter the type of crops he/she wants to grow in that soil and according to that the user will get the suggestions about it.
